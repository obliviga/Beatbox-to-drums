Beatbox → Drums

Record your beatbox. Hear it back as drums.

Press Record and beatbox — “B” kick · “Pss” snare · “Ts” hi-hat

One tap = one take with your own key (a few cents each). Your beat’s audio is sent to the AI provider for this feature only. Relay setup takes ~2 minutes: see worker/relay.js in the repo.

Runs in your browser — audio never leaves your device, except when you tap ✨ Restyle.
